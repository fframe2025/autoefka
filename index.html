About Us

Your Trusted Car Service Since 2011

At AutoEfka, we provide high-quality technical service, repair, and diagnostics of all makes of passenger cars in Klaipėda. We prepare cars for technical inspection and provide expert consultations on issues related to the technical condition and malfunctions.

We professionally perform assigned tasks, always working quickly and flawlessly. Our team takes the time to understand the expectations of each client, ensuring complete satisfaction with our services.

With over a decade of experience in the automotive service industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, expertise, and customer-focused service.
